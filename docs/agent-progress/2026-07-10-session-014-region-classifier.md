## Classifier Rules Added

- Strong Indonesia signals include:
  - `indonesia`
  - `indo`
  - `jakarta`
  - `bandung`
  - `surabaya`
  - `rupiah`
  - `mahal banget`
  - `anak dev indo`
  - `programmer indonesia`
  - `developer indonesia`
  - `kantor gue`
- Additional mixed/local signals include:
  - `gw`
  - `gue`
  - `lo`
  - `boros token`
  - `ngebantu`
  - `tapi`
  - `pakai`
  - `buat`
- English/global default is used when there are no Indonesia signals and the text has enough English/global context or length.
- `unknown` is used for short or low-confidence text such as a bare tool name.
